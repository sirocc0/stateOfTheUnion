The growth of target date funds brings increased demand for flexibility and choice. As LifePath®, BlackRock’s pioneering target date fund, prepares to mark its 25th anniversary in 2018, we look ahead at the trends driving change. Target date funds continue to grow because they are an excellent option for both plan sponsors and participants, creating a highly efficient way to manage investing for a heterogeneous population across an entire timeline. At the same time, there has never been a greater choice in terms of implementation, asset allocation and glidepath design.
